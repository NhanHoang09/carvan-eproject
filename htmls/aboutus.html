About Us

Company CARVAN is an automobile Joint Ventures founded in Semtember, 1995. The total investment capital was 89.6 million USD based on the capital contributions from CARVAN Motor Corporation (70%), Engine and Agricultural Machinery Corporation (20%) and KUO Pte.Ltd (10%).


Being one of the first automobile Joint Ventures, CARVAN never stops making efforts to develop with sustainably. CARVAN has been incessantly providing high quality products and perfect customer services to bring the highest satisfaction to our customers as well as positively contributing to the development of the automobile industry.

Since our establishment, CARVAN has incessantly developed not only in our sales but also in our production scale. The capacity of more than 30,000 units/year (two working shifts a day) and the accumulated sales of CARVAN reaching more than 695,424 units. All our products have a large market share in the market. With 11 employees since the first days, the current number of employees of CARVAN has reached over 1,900 and more than 8,800 employees working in 74 Dealers/Dealer's Branches nationwide.


Besides our business, CARVAN always puts much effort into the contribution to the state budget by fulfilling the duty of a taxpayer, as well as contributing to the sustainable development of the community and society with various long term, practical and meaningful activities.

Throughout the history of establishment and development, with all our efforts from CARVAN employees, dealers and suppliers, CARVAN has made great achievements and continuously improved itself to fulfill the mission of our customers and contribute significantly to automobile market and society. With these achievements, CARVAN seems to be one of the most successful FDI companies.
